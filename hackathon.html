Come One, Come All!

On November 22, 2014, CSWN will be hosting in association with Microsoft a 12-hour hackathon. Students of all disciplines are encouraged and welcomed to come!

Starting at 7:30am, the event will run until approximately 9:30 pm.

Don't miss out on the opportunity to brainstorm with other kids and create something you can call your own! Not only is this a great way to enhance your coding skills, it's also a fantasstic place to meet new people. You can come with a group of friends, or on your own and we'll find you a team.

Mentors will be available to all students to help in the process, and at the end of the day a panel of judges will evaluate what you've created. Prizes will be provided by Microsoft

If you'd like to participate (and who wouldn't?), fill out the form to the right. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at: cswn@cs.purdue.edu. We hope to see you there!
